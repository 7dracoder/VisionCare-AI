Departments

Eye care involves specialized departments, each focusing on different aspects of vision health to ensure comprehensive care

  • Ophthalmology
  • Optometry
  • Optical Dispensing
  • Retina and Vitreous
  • Pediatric Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology

Focuses on the medical and surgical treatment of eye diseases, including vision correction, cataract surgery, and conditions like glaucoma and retinal disorders.

Our network includes expert ophthalmologists who are highly skilled in treating a wide range of eye conditions, from cataracts to complex retinal diseases. They perform advanced surgical procedures and provide ongoing care to help restore and maintain your vision.

Optometry

Provides primary vision care, including eye exams, the prescription of glasses or contact lenses, and the detection of vision problems and eye diseases

We collaborate with top optometrists who are dedicated to providing comprehensive eye exams, prescribing the right corrective lenses, and identifying potential eye health issues early on. Their expertise ensures accurate diagnoses and personalized care tailored to your needs.

Optical Dispensing

Specializes in fitting, adjusting, and providing eyewear (glasses, contact lenses) based on prescriptions from optometrists or ophthalmologists

Our optical dispensers are professionals who specialize in fitting and adjusting eyewear. They work with high-quality materials to ensure that your glasses and contact lenses are perfectly suited to your prescription and comfort.

Retina and Vitreous

Deals with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the retina, such as diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and retinal detachment

We have strong ties with leading retina specialists who focus on diagnosing and treating retinal diseases such as diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and retinal detachment. These doctors provide expert care for serious conditions that affect the back of the eye.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Focuses on diagnosing and treating eye conditions in children, such as amblyopia (lazy eye), strabismus (crossed eyes), and congenital eye disorders

We connect you with pediatric ophthalmologists who specialize in treating eye conditions in children. Whether it’s addressing lazy eye (amblyopia) or correcting crossed eyes (strabismus), our specialists ensure that your child’s eye health is in capable hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries answered! Find quick solutions to common questions about our services and eye care practices

How can I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment through our online scheduling system or you can also use our assistant to book an appointment for your ease. Simply select a convenient time, provide your details, and confirm.

What symptoms should prompt me to see an eye care professional?

Symptoms like blurry vision, eye strain, redness, pain, or sudden vision changes indicate you should consult an eye care specialist promptly.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment using our platform. Log in to your account, select your appointment, and make the necessary changes.

How often should I get my eyes checked?

For adults, a comprehensive eye exam is recommended every 1–2 years, or more frequently if you have existing conditions like diabetes or a family history of eye diseases.

Is my personal and medical data secure on your platform?

Absolutely! We prioritize data security and comply with healthcare regulations to ensure your information is encrypted and protected.

Do you provide services for children’s eye care?

Yes, we have pediatric ophthalmologists who specialize in treating children’s eye conditions like lazy eye and crossed eyes.
